
#### Milestone 36.1: Pattern Category ŌĆö `decorators` (TS/JS)

<!-- milestone-meta

**Scope:** Introduce the `decorators` pattern category and wire its TypeScript /
JavaScript half. Decorators are the defining structural idiom of NestJS, Angular,
TypeORM, MikroORM, and class-validator: `@Injectable()`, `@Component({...})`,
`@Entity()`, `@Column()`, `@Get('/')`, `@IsString()`. Detection is **node-kind
only** (`decorator`) ŌĆö broad collection in the spirit of `class_hierarchy`, where
every decorator counts and decorator-shape entropy is the signal, not a curated
allowlist. **Requires a parsing-layer change:** `decorator` is not currently in
the TS/JS adapters' `PATTERN_KINDS`.

**Why this milestone exists:** In framework-heavy TS, decorators carry most of the
wiring intent (DI, routing, ORM mapping, validation). Their prevalence and shape
variance make them a high-value convention-drift bucket, and the user's stack
(NestJS/Angular-class frameworks) leans on them heavily.

**Deliverables:**
- Add `"decorator"` to `PATTERN_KINDS` in `crates/sdivi-lang-typescript/src/extract.rs`
  and `crates/sdivi-lang-javascript/src/extract.rs` (JS decorators are Stage-3 /
  TS-transpiled; collect if the grammar emits them).
- Create `crates/sdivi-patterns/src/queries/decorators.rs`:
  `NODE_KINDS: &[&str] = &["decorator"]`, no `matches_callee` (node-kind only).
- Register `decorators` in `ALL_CATEGORIES`, `category_for_node_kind`, and
  `sdivi-core::categories::CATALOG_ENTRIES`.
- Update `docs/pattern-categories.md` (canonical list + TS/JS node-kind table).

**Detection:**
| Language | Node kind | Examples |
|---|---|---|
| TS / JS | `decorator` | `@Injectable()`, `@Component({...})`, `@Get('/u')`, `@IsString()` |

note in `MIGRATION_NOTES.md`. `snapshot_version` stays `"1.0"`.
